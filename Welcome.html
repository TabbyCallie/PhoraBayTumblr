Server Rules:

  1. Do not be a dick. We’re all here to have fun, and most of the rules come down to just being decent to each other.
  2. Listen to the hierarchy, it is there for a reason. These people know what they’re talking about. While discussion and feedback are important, there will always be a final say!
  3. Any kind of discrimination won’t be accepted. Yes, that includes racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia etc. And for the love of god please do not intentionally trigger people.
  4. Some roleplay rules:
    1. Do not write for your opponent. You do not know if they have a trick up their sleeve against your attack.
    2. Do not be attacking someone much weaker than yourself, get OOC permission from either staff or the person involved.
    3. GM’s have the final say in arguments. If you feel like they’ve got something personal against you, take it to admins. Do not start a toxic argument, that will not reflect nicely on you.
    4. To keep formatting cohesive we will be using:
      • Italics for actions and what is happening.
      • ‘Italics and single quote for thoughts.’
      • ‘Italics, bold and single quotes from counter/secondary thoughts (if necessary)’
      • “Quotes for speech.”
  5. This server is mainly geared towards non-lethal combat. We do have people however that have killed, and will have an order for their death, those people are marked as Villains. If they’re known well enough, lethal force may be permitted. If you’re unsure ask a GM.

Our various staff members are here to help run the server:

@Game Masters Run events, roleplay NPC's and make decisions on outcomes in disputed roleplays.
@Lore Folk Constantly bring us new lore to work with and they'll happily take community suggestions
@XP Tyrant Read every single rp that happens on the server and reward you with xp every time.
@Mediator Keep a cool head in heated situations, the best people to tag if things heated on the server.

Our other main roles are:

@Heroes These are the good guys, wanting to stop crime and do the city proud.
@Vigilante Sometimes showing a bit of shade, but often just trying to do the right thing.
@Rogue People you can hire for money, or use their power for other financial gains.
@Felons Start to get into the shadier stuff, these are people who don't kill but commit crimes regularly.
@Villains The really bad people, often murders or terrorists.
@Student Underage roleplayers who attend school.

As with most servers, any problems with other members should be taken up to a staff member, and other issues (including about the staff) to me. If you have a problem with me, you can talk to anyone on the team, and keep in mind that we won’t be mad at you for expressing constructive feedback, as long as it doesn’t turn into excessive bitching about the person.
