Auralya

Name/Alias: Minerva Suzuli/Auralya

Age: 19

Mentality: Aurayla is a quiet, reserved girl who helps the innocent and enjoys taking retribution on the wicked.

Affiliation: Vigilante

Description: Hey there sweety, I'm here to help.

History: Minerva, Code Name: Auralya was raised in a small village that raised all of their local inhabitants to become ninjas. When her power showed itself, she quickly rose to the top of her class and graduated early due to the benefit of her power. Graduating 3 years early, she decided to take a hiatus from her duties and learn what it means to possess powers and what should be done with them. She traveled various lands, helping people discreetly and knocking out villains to deliver them to the cops. After her three year hiatus, she returned home and they told her of a city known as Phora Bay that can use her help. She accepts their mission and travels to Phora Bay in order to help the police there and save innocent lives.

Resources: Auralya has a small house with a garage on the south side of the city and a bank account. With 2k ollars that auto pays her bills but she has no access to otherwise and a brand new motorcycle that she uses for travel.

Equipment: She carries on her at all times 10 kunai knives and a tanto.

Skills: Lock-picking, hand to hand combat, basic parkour, pinpoint accuracy with thrown weapons, excellent ability to hide, and extreme proficiency with her trusty tanto.

Power: Flash Mirror Images: the user can summon two images of herself that can mimic simple moves and be controlled.

  • Mirror images last 5 turns, have a 5 turn cooldown, can travel up to 15 meters away from her, and cannot hurt themselves by any means (they can jump off a building or shoot themselves.) It takes a minor beating or a stab/gunshot to destroy them and causes them to explode in a shower of sparkles that functions as a smokescreen of sparkles.
  • Mirror Images can handle small weaponry such as small blades and kunai knives as well as small guns such as pistols and small SMG's and do the same damage as Aurayla.

Auralya also has the ability to trade places with her images using an ability.
Mirror Transfer: Auralya can trade places with her Mirror Images on a 4 turn cooldown. This transfer is instant and can happen in motion, but both the image and Auralya lose all momentum. This also applies to if the image is jumping or falling. She must have full vision of the image for this to work.

Versatility: Flash Mirror Images can mimic can throw kunai knives in synchronicity with Auralya letting her throw three kunai instead of just one. She can also use the mirror images to lead her attackers astray if she runs one way while they run another.

Demo: If she is fighting a thug on the street she can use her quirk to flash summon 2 copies of herself and all three will throw a kunai knife at full human damage
