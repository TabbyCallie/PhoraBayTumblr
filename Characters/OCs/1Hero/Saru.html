Saru

Name/Alias: Yamazaru Shizen/Saru

Age: 25

Mentality: Views fighting as entertainment, even against enemies. Gets bored easily outside of combat. Good Natured at heart and will fight to protect those to weak to do so, doesn't like putting others in danger. Can be a bit egotistical when it comes to his own ability.

Affiliation: Hero

Description: He has thick red unkempt hair swept back out of the face, slim athletic build, fair skinned. Saru wears monk beads as a necklace. He has patches of hair in various parts of his body, shoulders, chest, feet, hands, muttonchops, chin( because of the monkey features.) Has a monkey tail, same color as his hair, but tries to hide it when possible.

History: Yamazaru was born to Japanese parents in a small village in the mountains of China. His earliest days were recalled by his parents, a baby boy that resembled a monkey with fur growing from unusual parts of his body. As he grew older his parents found that the resemblance didn't stop with just looks, Yamazaru was agiler than any of the other kids, faster and stronger. He particularly liked to climb on top of their house, where he'd have to be coaxed down by his parents, or play tricks on his friends.
At age 13 Yamazaru was becoming more monkey-like. His parents began to find him more unmanageable, his playful behavior often resulted in mischevious acts, like taking something that didn't belong to him. His playfulness mixed with his above average strength proved difficult to deal with for most people, losing his friends that he used to play with through one too many injuries.
One fateful day, Yamazaru ran to town looking for someone to play with. He happened upon another kid his age who hesitantly agreed after some pestering from Yamazaru. During their game, Yamazaru had broken the child's arm by accident and ran home to his parents. The child's parents marched to his house and told Yamazaru's parents what he had done. Infuriated with his continuously growing unmanageable behavior, his parents contacted a temple, on a separate mountain from the village. The monks there took him in after inspecting the boy and his semblance to the monkey.
For 10 years Yamazaru was trained by the monks in combat and virtue. When in combat he is more akin to the monkey, absorbing little knowledge of their training, but skilled in his own way through his heightened agility, this is where he acquired his first bo staff. In a spar with another monk, he climbed his own staff that he placed on the ground and leaped off it, swinging the staff and breaking it across the monks back, when he landed his feet cracked the stone of the training hall. His punishment for this was to descend the mountain to gather fresh water in buckets, he had to do this enough times to supply every monk.
The lessons of virtue, however, stuck with Yamazaru. Learning the importance of peace, helping others, not to be greedy and he learned to follow karma, good things will come through good deeds. With his newfound inner peace, Yamazaru set out on his own journey. He wanted to use his abilities for good and to help people as much as he could. Finally finding his place to settle down, he became a hero and started fighting crime. To Yamazaru this was the best good deed he could do, because he got to fight!

Resources: Hero's income.

Equipment: Custom made 4.5 kg steel Bo staff that has 3 extendable settings.

  • Collapsed: 1 meter
  • Normal: 2 meters
  • Extended: 2.5 meters
  • Staff durability: 20kN

Skills: Novice Martial art skills, climbing, jumping, swinging.

Power: Monkey King: Yamazaru's monkey attributes make him part human, part monkey. Not only effecting his appearance but enhancing his physical attributes as well, making him faster, stronger, more nimble and agile.

  • Punch: 12kN
  • Kick: 15kN
  • Staff: 15kN
  • Run: 50 km/h
  • Jump: 3 meters vertically, 4 meters horizontally
  • Can lift 200 kg (two hands.)
  • Can easily climb up things like trees, lightpoles, posts, etc. etc.
  • His monkey-like attributes allow him to do things like land from heights of 9 meters safely.

Versatility: Yamazaru uses his agility in combat, jumping and swinging around the battlefield to train and gain the advantage against his opponent, his staff gives him a bit more reach than a fist and could even be thrown. He can use his staff to prop himself up like a pole for a small amount of time, just long enough to jump off of.

Demo: Yamazaru beamed gleefully at the villain as he hung from the rafters of an old warehouse. "Im having fun! aren't you?" He shouted down toward the man who couldnt reach him from his position. "Lets play some more!" With that he leaped down in front of the man, landing onto the ground and lunging at him with a staff strike.
