Reversal

Name/Alias: Sero Fernandes/Reversal

Age: 26

Mentality: Cold and ruthless but not a psychopath. He isn't afraid to kill but he doesn't kill if unnecessary. He lives for himself and cares little what others think of him. He just cares about his survival and owns well being.

Affiliation: Rogue (More on the bad side.)

Description:

Ignore the red dude behind him

History: N/A

Resources: 12k in current currency, owns an apartment on 8th floor in the city center. Works as a barber and gets 2.8k a month.

Equipment: None.

Skills: Good at acting and getting out of sticky situations. Knows few self-defense skills but hasn't ever studied any martial art officially. Also is a good dresser.

Power: Vector manipulation.
Sero can manipulate vectors. He can change the direction of max 5 incoming separate vectors completely. Max speed of a Vector he can manipulate is 35 m/s. Beyond that will be impossible. Upon changing direction, they lose 30% of their initial speed. Sero also can slow down any incoming vector by 70%. The range of manipulation is 10 m radius. He can also speed up any vector that moves in opposite direction from Sero by 50%. Once he has changed the direction of 5 vectors his ability goes on 3 turn cooldown.
Absolute stop: Sero goes in overload mod for 1 turn where he can freeze any vector that enters his range no matter how quick it is. After that turn is over he goes on 5 turn cooldown.
Drawback: Sero cannot manipulate any vector he doesn't see so attacking his blind spot is the easiest way to win. Also sending more than 5 different objects at once will overload him and few of those will surely go through. He also cannot affect spectral melee weapons.
Retrieval: Makes up to 3 small portals to retrieve up to 3 items that she touched in the last 3 turns. It takes .5 seconds for the portal to open and 0.5 seconds for it to become active. If the item hasn't been touched in the last 3 turns then she can only make 1 portal and it will take double the time to open and become active. This portal won't work if someone is holding the item she tries to retrieve or if it is attached to something. Cooldown 2 turns.

Versatility: He has extremely good defense and in some case offense but is lacking mobility.

Demo: 3 thugs attack Sero on the street. 1 swings a bat at him at full speed so Sero reverses the bat and hits the thug in the head. The other one throws a knife at Sero so he changes the angle of the knife and directs it into the leg of the third thug. He then picks up the bat and throws it towards the second thug while increasing the speed by 50%.
