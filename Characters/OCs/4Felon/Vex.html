Vex

Name/Alias: Adrienne Bellamy/Vex

Age: 22

Mentality: Confident, prideful, obsessive, impulsive, and incredibly vain, Vex is a manipulative hedonist and sadomasochist. Seemingly on a whim, she takes a fervent interest in other persons (especially parahumans) and stalks them. Sometimes her attention is harmless or playful, but more often than not she will torment them or attempt to lure and/or bait the individual into an ambush before torturing them. Likes expensive things, especially wine.

Affiliation: Felon

Description: Adrienne has fine and beautiful features, that further accentuated by black hair, jade-green eyes, and a slender 'hourglass' physique. Put simply, she's drop-dead gorgeous, and being as vain and prideful as she is, she doesn't hide that she knows it.
In her cape persona as Vex, Adrienne wears a demonic mask as well as a form-fitting and very flattering black dress

Demonic Mask

Black Dress

History: Originally from France, she came to America after she met an influential supervillain vacationing in his civilian identity and became instantly and utterly smitten with the man. (One might even suspect foul play from a power...) He didn't feel the same way for her, but led her on as though he did to satisfy his own selfish interests, eventually taking her back to America with him but considering her to be essentially nothing more than arm candy - while to Adrienne, it seemed as though this was nothing less than the start of a new life with her soulmate. Now back in his element, it didn't take long before the villain tired of his charade, and while conducting business in Phora he left Adrienne sitting alone in a café halfway through a romantic lunch after excusing himself to take a phone call.

Resources: None, she was left with nothing but the clothes on her back and the money in her pocket after being left alone.

Equipment: Vex carries a tactical light small enough to fit in her palm, a whistle, a taser, a small can of pepper spray, smelling salts, a lighter, a set of torturer's tools, zip-ties and/or handcuffs, a karambit, and a cat o' nine tails.

Skills: Speaks English, French, a little German, lies like a pro and spots them just as easily. She’s very perceptive, allowing her to be good at reading people and situations, knows how to escape cuffs and zip-ties, familiar with the human anatomy — especially pressure points, has some limited training with a karambit. Lastly, she’s a skilled violinist.

Power: Vex can target one person through sight up to a max range of 25m and cause their senses to become 1/4x less sensitive to her or objects on her person, or up to 10x more sensitive to her or objects on her person. (For reference, a dog's hearing is about 4x that of a human's.) Vex's power doesn't actually amplify/dull the sensations she causes but instead amplifies/dulls the perception of the sensation. So she could make a firework deafeningly loud, but she wouldn't actually cause any damage to someone's hearing and deafen them unless it normally would. Vex is able to selectively target specific senses to enhance, and line of sight doesn't need to be maintained for the power to work after she chooses a target. Enhanced or extra senses can be affected as well, and do not hinder the potency of the sensitivity change.

Versatility: Versatility is fairly low, though the power has a few tricks that can be leveraged outside of the most direct use of the power such as whispering across a room, or making her harder/easier to find in a crowd.

Demo: Before he was writhing in the dirt, he was a lawyer. The setup was simple, Adrienne had invited him to come out to a bar, and had enhanced his senses to make her perfume so, so easy to smell - the best part? It was the same kind as his deceased wife's. She had him basically wrapped around her finger the whole night, and when she suggested moving somewhere 'private', his alcohol-seeped mind didn't even allow him a second thought. A couple streets down, and an alley or two later she'd taken him down with a heel to the thigh. As he lay crying in pain and sobbing, Vex fished out a knife from a well-concealed thigh sheath...
