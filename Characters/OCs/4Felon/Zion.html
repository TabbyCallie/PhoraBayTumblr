Zion

Name/Alias: Johnny Brandenburg/Zion

Age: 25

Mentality: He has a possessive mentality and wishes for everything to be in his control. Because of that, he gets a deep desire to acquire what he wants and he will use any means to achieve his goals. It could be a friendly and caring act, or it could be a threatening act, possibly a blackmail. He is sly and manipulative and his methods of doing things might change from situation to situation.

Affiliation: Felon

Description: As mentioned in his mentality, he is very manipulative as well as obsessive over the things he wants and possessive over the things he has. His mentality to dominate can even be reflected in his ability Zone of Control from which goes one of his personal favorite sayings “World domination begins with 30 meters.”

History: He started developing his powers since a young age, ever since he started getting possessive thoughts. At around the age of 10, he became a leader of his childhood friend gang. After a year that gang ended up influencing all similar children groups in the neighborhood. That was his first taste of power and the feeling was intoxicating to him, thus he started wanting more. At age of 18, he enrolled in a rather prestigious university to study psychology, politics, and law through “fair” means and next year he became a student council president. The votings were “totally legit” and no bribery or vote rigging was involved. After graduating at the age of 23 he migrated to Phora Bay and started setting sight on the place. “This place is interesting and I want it” is what he said after a year living here and thus started working on taking control of the underworld under the name of Zion, while his public face was a lawyer named Johnny.

Resources: $3000 a month from his lawyer wage, as well as whatever income his controlled organizations as Zion brings him. A motorcycle and a reasonable size apartment.

Equipment: Three 10kg steel balls as well as three daggers on him.

Skills: Manipulation, psychology, law.

Power: Zone of Control. Zion can create a domain-like zone of 30 meters in diameter either around him or he can prepare a small zone sphere which he can throw at 10m/s which expands either on impact or when Zion wishes. Zion needs to wait a turn to use the zone. Zone's cooldown is 1/4th of the time it was used with 2 turn cooldown as a minimum. This zone has various properties which can be summed up in 2 categories: the inside of the zone and the membrane of the zone. Inside zone abilities - immunity to mental effects as well, telekinesis (affectable weight limit as well as singular lifting weight limit is 1000kg).
Telekinesis weight to speed ratio:

  • 1000 kg -> 5 m/s
  • 500 kg -> 6 m/s
  • 250 kg -> 8 m/s
  • 100 kg -> 10 m/s
  • 50 kg -> 15 m/s
  • 10 kg -> 30 m/s

Membrane abilities - can slow and weaken any attack which passes through, the slow effect is proportionate to the attack’s weight and Zion’s TK speed limit, this is a reactionary passive effect. It can change the way membrane looks.

Versatility: It is a very versatile ability, providing both offenses with telekinesis by sending heavy objects at an enemy’s face, defense thanks to the nature of telekinesis as well as the membrane as well as utility gained from telekinesis and who knows what his zone will be able to do later on.

Demo: Zion sees a serial killer pulling out a knife and starting charging at him, thus he sets up a zone around himself. The serial killer gets cautious by the ominous looking sphere in front of him, thus deciding not to enter it and threw a knife aiming at Zion at 20m/s, however once the knife passed through the membrane it stopped completely because it’s speed didn’t exceed 30m/s. Zion looked at the dagger pointing at him and made it point towards the criminal, and sent it flying back at him at 30m/s, once the knife left the zone, it’s speed was somewhat retained thanks to inertia, though the speed kept decreasing slightly yet it was still enough to make that knife stab into SKs throat.
