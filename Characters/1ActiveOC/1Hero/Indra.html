Indra

Name/Alias: Vali Arkwright/Indra

Age: 23

Mentality: Self-Centered. Egoistic. Works for no one but himself. Would stab someone for his gain.
Born royalty of a small European kingdom unheard of in most parts of the world to most but those who are actively involved in deep politics. He knows he doesn't own everything, yet he takes what he wants when he can afford it.

Affiliation: Hero, by occupation. Lawful Evil at heart. Unwilling to reveal his true colors, tries to act Lawful Good.

Description:

Luscious Blonde locks, an air of nobility, and the rich blue eyes expected of a European Prince adorn this man's fair face, seemingly never allowing anything to stain his appearance, no matter how uncared for.

No matter how twisted his expressions are, they never seem to give justice to truly how evil this man can be.

History: Born as the younger son of the King of a minuscule European Kingdom, he had always hoped to succeed his elder brother and one day take the throne. They were both trained in what was expected of royalty, both of them smart, however, his elder brother always surpassed him.
But he had one flaw... he was too kind, too trusting, too defenseless. One day, fed up with everyone around him treating him like he was inferior to his elder brother, he set in motion his plan to assassinate his elder brother. Unfortunately, the guards were too well trained for Vali to succeed. When the King heard of this, he exiled Vali from the kingdom, covering up the incident to save face for the royal family.
Vali was forced to go away to another city far away, allowed to come back only when he had matured into a fine young man, worthy of being praised as a member of the Royal Family.
The quickest way? It was the become a hero and commit good deeds, just so he could improve his image, and pretend to see the error of his ways.

Resources: Hero's Income. Some maintenance money from his family when needed to sustain his lifestyle.

Equipment: The weapons he summons.

Skills: Well-versed in playing the piano. Learned Martial Arts as part of being a part of the Royal Family, highly physically trained, as a way of surpassing his brother.

Power: Weapons Summoning
The user summons weapons that only he can use. He can at max have 1 weapon out at the same time and it takes 10 seconds to shift between weapons. He can't shift weapons again the turn after he shifted or that same turn.
Shield: A 1.5x1.5m great shield that can move at 10m/s and can withstand up to 10kN of attacks (armor). The shield can be moved by the user's mind. It cannot lift anything, but he can sustain at least 90 KGs without lowering altitude. It has a range of 10 meters, after which the telepathic link fails.
The user can turn the shield into a barrier (blue barrier for good, red for evil) of max 25m^3. That can withstand up to 15kN before shattering. The barrier cannot be moved. The barrier is limited to basic shapes. The barrier can be cast within 10 meters radius of the shield.
The shield can be transformed into a suit of armor (6kN) which allows flight at 3 m/s. This can be used to make it effectively weightless, allowing Vali to not tire out as much.
If the barrier breaks the weapon will fade away, taking 2 turns to be summoned. In normal form, the shield can be pierced without fading.
4-swords: Summons a set of four one-handed swords and causes the user will grow 2 temporary hands that will also fade away when the swords fade away. These arms cannot be used to interact with anything other than the swords. The swords add 3kN cutting and 2kN punching strength.
Staff: The user will stun any limbs that the staff slams into for 2 turns and if it hit's someone's torso it will be able to half their speed for 1 turn. If the user hits them once more on the torso after that the opponent will be stunned for 1 turn. The hit of the staff also pushes people 2 meters back. The staff doesn't stun the user.
Mask: Vali can choose to don a blue mask on his face, although he doesn't do it, seeing as he's hiding it, for when he needs it the most. Unlike the other weapons, Vali can keep this summoned along with his other summons, without needing to de-summon other summons.
