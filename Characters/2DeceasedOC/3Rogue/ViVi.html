ViVi

Name / Alias: Victoria Victoire/ViVi

Age: 23

Mentality: “Better to speak the ugly truth than say a pretty lie.” She basically is very blunt and hates withholding the truth even if it means making someone feel bad. ViVi is a very laid back individual who just happens to drink more coffee than is usually healthy for a normal person. She recently bought a café but she’s not exactly sure on how to run some aspects of it and sometimes gives delicious coffee away for free, if not dirt cheap.

Affiliation: Rogue

Description: ViVi generally wears very old clothes that look like hand-me-downs and has no scars. She constantly looks like she’s in a bad mood even if she’s in a good mood.

History: Born in a small unimportant town, ViVi has always had her manifestation of her soul by her side but she’s never been able to find the right name for it and just calls it Mani for the time being. After she moved to Phora Bay, she bought a café, thinking she could run it but has had no luck as of yet. She hasn’t done anything particularly bad but she hasn’t done anything good either.

Resources: The café near city gardens is pretty much her only property besides some clothes and the things necessary to run the café. She’s lower middle class and she lives in the attic of her café to save money. $40,000/year.

Equipment: Nothing but the clothes on her back plus a baseball cap.

Skills: Hot drink making, procrastinating, and being sarcastic.

Power: Can summon a manifestation of her soul that can heat anything it touches up to 300 degrees celsius. Mani can’t go beyond a meter away from ViVi and it has to touch the object to heat it up.

  • Can heat up things to 300 C max
  • Takes 30 seconds to heat up to maximum temperature and 10 seconds per 100 C.
  • 7kN Punch
  • 8kN Kick
  • 7kN Durability
  • Power upon contact heats up a 3 meter radius.

Note: Mani can’t cool things down, only heat things up.

Versatility: She mostly uses her power to heat up coffee, tea and other hot drinks, but she can use it in combat situations if she has to. When in a combat situation, Mani can boil the victim’s blood until they pass out or die.

Demo: As ViVi walked through the alley, two thugs cut off her escape. “Oi, whadya think ya doing cutting me off? It’s bad enough in traffic, but now ya city folk are cutting me off on my way to work!” The two thugs looked at each other for a moment before laughing at her. One pulled out a switch blade and went for ViVi’s right arm. “Oh sod off ya fucking luddite.” She said as Mani appeared and grabbed the man’s arm and boiled his blood.
